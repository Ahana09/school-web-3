Won 1st place in MSLTA JUDO Competition

Ahana Anup Shah of Class 4 B won a Gold Medal at the Mumbai Games Judo Competition Under-12 Girls and a Silver Medal at the SFA Judo Competition Under-12 Girls

EducationWorld ranks DAIS the No. 1 International School in India

Dhirubhai Ambani International School has been honoured with the Excellence in Academics award at the national level in the very first edition of the NDTV Education Awards. Besides this national level recognition as the best school in the category of Excellence in Academics, DAIS also received the Excellence in Academics award and the Excellence in Sports award at the western zone regional level. This recognition from a leading channel like NDTV is a fitting tribute to the visionary leadership of Mrs Nita Ambani, Founder & Chairperson of the School and to the School's dedicated and committed team of teachers. For a school that focuses on holistic development of children in an inclusive environment, this accolade will surely inspire it to set new benchmarks of excellence in the years ahead.
